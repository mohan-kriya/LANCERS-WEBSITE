End-to-End Contract Manufacturing for Sustainable Agri-Inputs

Kriya Ltd offers a complete contract manufacturing platform that spans R&D, formulation, microbial fermentation, and eco-friendly packaging. We partner with agri-tech innovators worldwide to produce residue-conscious, regulatory-compliant, and scalable natural solutions.

Our Core Capabilities

Supercritical CO₂ Extraction

High-purity actives from botanical sources with no solvent residues — ensuring eco-safe and consistent results.

Solvent & Aqueous Extraction

Scalable extraction technologies for multiple bioactive fractions, optimized for purity and yield.

Microbial Fermentation & Metabolite Extraction

Specialized production of beneficial microbes, spores, and metabolites under controlled conditions.

Formulation & Packing

Customizable dosage forms: EC, SC, WP, WSP, tablets, and RTU sprays, tailored to global regulatory needs.

R&D & Analytical Services

Comprehensive stability studies, assays, and documentation for certification and compliance.

Our Engagement Model

  1. Scope: Define objectives, formulations, and target markets.
  2. Feasibility: Conduct small-scale trials to validate technical and economic potential.
  3. Pilot: Optimize process parameters through intermediate-scale runs.
  4. Scale-Up: Move to commercial production under robust QA/QC systems.
  5. Supply: Deliver consistent, compliant, and traceable production globally.
