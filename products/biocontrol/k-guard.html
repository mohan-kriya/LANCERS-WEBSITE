K-Guard — Pongamia-Based Biopesticide

K-Guard is a pongamia-based biopesticide formulated with natural karanjin compounds. It provides both insecticidal and antifungal protection, delivering a residue-conscious and eco-friendly approach to pest and disease management. Available in EC and WSP formulations, K-Guard empowers growers with a reliable, multipurpose bio-solution for sustainable agriculture.

K-Guard Variants

K-Guard (EC)

Liquid EC formulation rich in karanjin for insect and fungal suppression.

K-Rix (WSP)

Overview

K-Guard utilizes pongamia oil’s active component, karanjin, to target both insect pests and fungal pathogens. It offers broad protection across crops while reducing dependency on chemical pesticides. Its natural formulation ensures minimal environmental impact and residue-free produce.

Mode of Action

Karanjin acts as a contact and stomach poison in insects, disrupting feeding and respiration. It also inhibits fungal spore germination and hyphal growth, providing dual-action efficacy.

Key Features

  • Dual insecticidal and antifungal activity for multi-target crop protection.
  • Eco-friendly formulation based on natural karanjin from pongamia oil.
  • Safe for beneficial insects and pollinators.
  • Non-phytotoxic and compatible with most biostimulants and foliar sprays.
  • Residue-conscious solution supporting organic and IPM systems.

Compatibility

  • Compatible with most organic foliar feeds and biostimulants.
  • Avoid mixing with strong alkalis or oxidizing agents.
  • Perform jar test before combining with other inputs.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: Vegetables, cotton, pulses, cereals, oilseeds, fruits, and plantation crops.

Target Pests: Aphids, thrips, whiteflies, mites, and powdery mildew.

Benefits: Dual-action pest and fungal control with natural karanjin-based mode of action; ideal for integrated and organic farming.
