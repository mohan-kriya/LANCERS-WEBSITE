Ecoza — Neem-Based Biopesticide

Ecoza is a neem-based biopesticide formulated with standardized azadirachtin fractions. It provides broad-spectrum protection against sucking and chewing pests, combining systemic and antifeedant action for safe, sustainable crop protection. Available in EC and WSP formulations across four variants — Ecoza Max, Ecoza Ace, Ecoza Rix, and Ecoza Pro.

Ecoza Variants

Ecoza Max (3.0% EC)

High-strength azadirachtin EC for heavy pest pressure and export-oriented crops.

Overview

Ecoza provides broad-spectrum control of aphids, thrips, whiteflies, and other soft-bodied insects. Its bioactive azadirachtin compounds disrupt insect molting, feeding, and reproduction, providing reliable protection with minimal environmental impact.

Key Features

  • Multiple strengths for varied crop and pest pressures.
  • Derived from pure neem seed extracts with standardized azadirachtin levels.
  • Safe for pollinators and beneficial organisms.
  • Organic-certifiable and residue-conscious.
  • Dual activity: IGR + Repellency + Antifeedant effect.

Compatibility

  • Compatible with most organic sprays and foliar feeds.
  • Avoid mixing with strong alkaline or oxidative inputs.
  • Perform a jar test before large-scale mixing.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: Vegetables, fruits, cotton, pulses, oilseeds, ornamentals, plantations.

Target Pests: Aphids, thrips, mealybugs, whiteflies, leaf miners, caterpillars.

Benefits: Fast-acting, natural pest control with no chemical residues, ideal for sustainable and export-compliant markets.
