Overview

Spindura provides effective control against chewing and sucking insects in vegetables, fruits, and ornamentals. It acts rapidly on insect nervous systems and offers residual activity that ensures long-lasting pest control while remaining safe for beneficial insects.

Mode of Action

Spinosad targets nicotinic acetylcholine receptors (nAChR) and GABA receptors in insects, leading to hyperexcitation, paralysis, and death. It provides both contact and ingestion toxicity, making it effective against a broad range of pests.

Key Features

  • Fermentation-derived active ingredient with natural origin.
  • Fast knockdown combined with residual action for long-lasting protection.
  • Highly effective against thrips, caterpillars, and leafminers.
  • Safe for pollinators when used as per label directions.
  • Compatible with IPM and organic farming systems.
