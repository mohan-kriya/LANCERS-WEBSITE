WeedX — Natural Fatty Acid Herbicide

WeedX is a natural, post-emergence herbicide derived from medium-chain fatty acids (capric and caprylic acids). It provides effective control of broadleaf weeds through contact action, ensuring clean and residue-free weed management in sustainable and organic systems.

WeedX Formulation

WeedX (EC Formulation)

Natural herbicidal concentrate providing rapid desiccation of unwanted vegetation without soil residues.

Download Product Sheet

Overview

WeedX is a natural herbicidal formulation designed for post-emergence control of weeds. It acts on plant cuticles, causing rapid dehydration and cell membrane disruption, resulting in visible effects within hours of application. Being biodegradable, it leaves no harmful residues and is safe for use in organic-certified farming.

Mode of Action

WeedX disrupts plant cuticle membranes and wax layers upon contact, leading to leakage of cellular contents and rapid desiccation. It acts non-selectively on actively growing green tissues and does not translocate, ensuring precise weed removal without affecting surrounding crops.

Key Features

  • Derived from natural fatty acids — environmentally safe and biodegradable.
  • Fast-acting contact herbicide with visible effects in 1–3 hours.
  • Non-selective, effective on a wide range of broadleaf weeds and grasses.
  • Leaves no residues and supports organic certification.
  • Ideal for inter-row and spot applications.

Compatibility

  • Not recommended for tank mixing with crop protectants or biostimulants.
  • Avoid spraying near desirable green plants due to non-selective action.
  • Use clean water and calibrated nozzles for uniform spray coverage.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Use: Inter-row weed management and post-emergence weed control.

Target Weeds: Broadleaf weeds, annual grasses, and unwanted vegetation in fallow fields or borders.

Benefits: Rapid, residue-free weed desiccation; safe for soil and environment; ideal for organic systems.
