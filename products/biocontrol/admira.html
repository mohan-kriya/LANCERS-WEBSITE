Overview

Admira offers a unique range of essential oil-based pest management solutions derived from plant-based bioactives. These formulations are effective against mites, aphids, thrips, and fungal leaf spots, providing a safe and eco-friendly pest control solution.

Mode of Action

The essential oils in Admira work by disrupting insect cell membranes, inhibiting feeding, and acting as repellents. They also possess antifungal properties, preventing spore germination and mycelial growth on leaves and stems.

Key Features

  • Multiple essential oil variants for flexible pest management.
  • Repellent, insecticidal, and antifungal properties combined in one product line.
  • Safe for beneficial insects and non-toxic to the environment.
  • Supports organic certification and residue-free farming.
  • Ideal for IPM and rotational use with other biocontrols.
