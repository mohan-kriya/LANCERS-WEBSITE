Margoshine — Neem Oil-Based Biopesticide

Margoshine offers neem oil–based pest control with the dual benefits of insect management and plant health enhancement. Its cold-pressed neem oil contains azadirachtin, salannin, and nimbin, providing a natural, residue-free alternative to synthetic pesticides. Available in 70% EC and 35% WSP formulations, Margoshine delivers reliable pest control in both conventional and organic farming systems.

Margoshine Variants

Margoshine

High-strength cold-pressed neem oil EC for broad-spectrum insect control and plant health improvement.

Overview

Margoshine combines insecticidal and antifungal properties of neem oil with plant growth-promoting compounds. It acts through contact, ingestion, and repellency while improving leaf gloss and vigor, making it ideal for integrated pest management (IPM) systems.

Mode of Action

Neem oil’s active triterpenoids (azadirachtin, salannin, nimbin) disrupt insect feeding and reproduction while smothering soft-bodied pests. It also interferes with fungal spore germination, providing broad-spectrum activity against mites, aphids, and fungal pathogens.

Key Features

  • Cold-pressed neem oil containing azadirachtin, salannin, and nimbin fractions.
  • Dual pest control and plant health enhancement in one product.
  • Non-toxic to beneficial organisms when used as recommended.
  • Organic-certifiable and eco-friendly alternative to chemicals.
  • Improves leaf sheen and photosynthetic efficiency.

Compatibility

  • Compatible with most foliar biostimulants and micronutrients.
  • Avoid tank mixing with alkaline or sulfur-based inputs.
  • Perform jar test before combining with other sprays.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: Vegetables, fruits, ornamentals, cotton, pulses, oilseeds, and plantation crops.

Target Pests: Aphids, thrips, whiteflies, mealybugs, mites, and fungal leaf pathogens.

Key Benefits: Natural multi-mode insect control, improved plant vitality, and residue-free performance for export crops.
