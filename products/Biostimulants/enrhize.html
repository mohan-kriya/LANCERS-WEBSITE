Overview

Enrhize contains viable spores of **Glomus species** (â‰¥1500 propagules/g), a beneficial root-colonizing fungus that forms symbiotic associations with plant roots. This partnership increases nutrient and water absorption efficiency while promoting plant resilience to abiotic stress factors like drought and salinity.

Mode of Action

The fungal spores germinate and penetrate plant roots, forming arbuscules within root cortical cells. These structures facilitate nutrient exchange between the fungus and plant, increasing phosphorus, nitrogen, and micronutrient uptake, while also improving root surface area and soil structure.
