Cropsia — Botanical Extract-Based Biostimulant

Cropsia is a biostimulant formulated with botanical extracts of Moringa and Adhatoda vasica, rich in natural antioxidants and plant growth compounds. It enhances crop vigor, promotes nutrient uptake, and supports stress resilience for improved yield and quality under challenging environments.

Cropsia Formulation

Cropsia (SL / WSP)

Botanical extract formulation combining Moringa and Adhatoda vasica for improved crop performance and plant health.

Download Product Sheet

Overview

Cropsia leverages the natural bioactivity of Moringa and Adhatoda vasica to promote photosynthesis, nutrient assimilation, and metabolic balance. Its antioxidant compounds protect plants from oxidative stress, improving vegetative growth and yield potential in various crops.

Mode of Action

Bioactive molecules from Moringa and Adhatoda extracts stimulate enzyme activation, enhance chlorophyll production, and regulate hormonal activity — resulting in improved plant vigor, better nutrient uptake, and enhanced stress resistance.

Key Features

  • Derived from Moringa and Adhatoda vasica botanical extracts.
  • Enhances chlorophyll synthesis and photosynthetic activity.
  • Improves stress tolerance and overall plant vigor.
  • Natural, residue-free formulation safe for organic farming.
  • Promotes balanced vegetative and reproductive growth.

Dosage & Dilution

Formulation Rate / Acre Dilution
SL / WSP 500–1000 ml / g 1.25–2.5 ml or g / L

Compatibility

  • Compatible with most foliar fertilizers and micronutrient solutions.
  • Avoid mixing with strong acids or alkaline chemicals.
  • Apply during early morning or evening for maximum efficiency.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: Vegetables, fruits, pulses, cereals, and plantation crops.

Benefits: Stimulates plant metabolism, enhances growth and productivity, improves plant resilience and nutrient efficiency for better yield outcomes.
