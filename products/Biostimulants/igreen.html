IGreen — Advanced Microbial Biostimulant

IGreen is a next-generation microbial biostimulant platform formulated with multi-strain beneficial bacteria. It enhances nutrient release, soil fertility, and crop resilience by integrating nitrogen-fixing, phosphorus-solubilizing, potassium-mobilizing, and disease-suppressive microorganisms. Designed for all crops, IGreen rejuvenates soil health and ensures long-term productivity through a balanced microbial ecosystem.

Overview

IGreen integrates synergistic microbial consortia that fix nitrogen, solubilize phosphorus, and mobilize potassium while enhancing stress tolerance and rhizosphere immunity. Each variant addresses specific soil and crop needs — from nutrient efficiency to disease resistance — making IGreen a complete soil vitality solution for sustainable agriculture.

Mode of Action

The microbial strains in IGreen colonize plant roots and the rhizosphere, secreting enzymes and organic acids that release locked nutrients. They produce metabolites and hormones that stimulate root growth, activate plant defense genes, and improve soil aggregation and moisture balance.

Key Features

  • Holistic nutrient cycling — fixes N, solubilizes P, and mobilizes K simultaneously.
  • Enhances root biomass, soil aggregation, and nutrient absorption.
  • Improves microbial biodiversity and organic carbon in soil.
  • Mitigates drought, salinity, and soil stress.
  • Safe, residue-free, and compatible with organic farming systems.

Compatibility & Application

  • Can be applied via soil drench, seed treatment, or fertigation systems.
  • Avoid direct mixing with fungicides or bactericides.
  • Reapply every 3–4 weeks during active crop growth for best results.
  • Store sealed in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: All field, horticultural, and plantation crops.

Benefits: Improves soil fertility, enhances stress resilience, reduces fertilizer dependency, and promotes long-term soil vitality.
