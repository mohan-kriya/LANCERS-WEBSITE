Overview

Mystica optimizes foliar spray performance by reducing surface tension and enhancing droplet spread on leaf surfaces. It allows better absorption of active ingredients, improving the performance of biocontrol, biostimulant, and foliar nutrient applications.

Mode of Action

Mystica contains natural surfactants that lower water surface tension, enabling spray droplets to spread uniformly and adhere firmly to plant tissues. This ensures better wetting, less runoff, and increased penetration of active ingredients into plant surfaces.

Key Features

  • Plant-derived, biodegradable wetting and spreading agent.
  • Improves spray coverage and uniformity on leaves.
  • Enhances absorption and utilization of foliar-applied inputs.
  • Reduces spray runoff and wastage.
  • Non-toxic, residue-free, and compatible with organic farming programs.
