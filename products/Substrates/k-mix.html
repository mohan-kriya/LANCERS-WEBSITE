K-Mix — Karanja Cake-Based Organic Substrate

K-Mix is an organic soil substrate formulated from **karanja cake**. It improves soil fertility, enhances microbial diversity, and provides natural suppression of nematodes and soil-borne pests. K-Mix is an eco-friendly alternative to synthetic soil conditioners for sustainable crop production.

K-Mix Formulation

K-Mix

Organic substrate made from karanja cake, rich in organic nitrogen and secondary metabolites for soil health and pest management.

Download Product Sheet

Overview

K-Mix is derived from **karanja (Pongamia pinnata)** seeds and serves as a natural soil conditioner and organic fertilizer. It enriches the soil with essential nutrients, organic matter, and beneficial microbial growth while suppressing soil nematodes and pathogens naturally.

Mode of Action

The bioactive compounds and fatty acids in karanja cake release secondary metabolites that inhibit nematode activity and soil-borne pathogens. Simultaneously, organic carbon supports beneficial microbial growth, improving soil structure and nutrient cycling.

Key Features

  • Rich in organic nitrogen and essential plant nutrients.
  • Provides natural nematode suppression and pest management.
  • Improves soil microbial biodiversity and structure.
  • Safe and eco-friendly alternative to chemical soil conditioners.
  • Enhances long-term soil fertility and plant vigor.

Dosage & Application

Formulation Rate / Acre Application
Granular / Powder 100–200 kg Apply to soil at planting or as top dressing

Compatibility

  • Compatible with organic manures and biofertilizers.
  • Avoid combining with synthetic nematicides or fungicides.
  • Incorporate into soil during field preparation for best results.

Target Use & Benefits

Recommended Crops: Vegetables, pulses, cereals, fruits, and plantation crops.

Benefits: Enriches soil fertility, suppresses nematodes, enhances microbial health, and promotes sustainable soil management.
