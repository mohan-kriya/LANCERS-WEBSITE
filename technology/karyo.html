Transforming Natural Actives into Scalable Crop Solutions

Karyo is Kriya’s advanced delivery platform for nature-derived actives. We take complex botanical and microbial molecules and convert them into stable, effective formulations ready for commercial-scale agriculture. Through encapsulation, stabilization, and optimized delivery, Karyo ensures that every product performs consistently across crops and climates.

Core Capabilities

Botanical Actives Formulation

Stabilization of essential oils, neem fractions, and bioactive extracts for enhanced shelf-life and performance.

Microbial Actives Formulation

High-viability formulations for spores, cells, and metabolites ensuring long-term product stability and efficacy.

Advanced Delivery (Encapsulation)

Micro and nano-encapsulation technologies for controlled release and improved compatibility across inputs.

Formulation Science

Balancing efficacy, stability, and shelf-life through formulation optimization and analytical validation.

Sustainability Focus

Use of eco-friendly excipients, biodegradable carriers, and sustainable packaging materials.

Target Audience

For Global Distributors & Agri-Tech Innovators

Karyo empowers international distributors, agri-tech firms, and crop-input companies to expand their portfolios with differentiated, premium formulations backed by cutting-edge delivery innovation.

Key Benefit

Karyo enables the transition from **lab-scale discovery** to **market-ready crop solutions**, ensuring consistent performance, regulatory compliance, and scalability — bridging innovation with real-world agricultural impact.
